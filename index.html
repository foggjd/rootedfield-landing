Orchard Management FAQ

What is RootedField?

RootedField is orchard management software that helps growers track trees, manage care schedules, monitor watering, map orchard layouts, and organize farm operations.

Can RootedField track individual trees?

Yes. RootedField allows users to track individual trees with records for health, location, activities, notes, and growth history.

Does RootedField support irrigation scheduling?

Yes. RootedField includes watering and irrigation scheduling tools to help growers plan and manage tree care.

Who is RootedField built for?

RootedField is built for orchardists, farmers, homesteaders, backyard growers, and teams managing fruit trees or orchard operations.
